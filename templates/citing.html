Citing the Project
  • Guidelines

“Research data should be freely accessible to everyone – for scientists as well as for the general public”

From which source?

According to SNSF principles, research data-sharing is a fundamental contribution to the impact, transparency and reproducibility of scientific research. The fagottino and tenoroon research team believes research data should be shared as openly as possible. In order to cite research material on this website, we recommend the following format:

For material with a named author:

Author, “Title”, Fagottini and tenoroons (2018), <web address>, accessed [date accessed]

FHNW University of Applied Sciences and Arts Northwestern Switzerland Schola Cantorum Basiliensis / Academy of Music

SNSF project “Fagottini and Tenoroons"

Leonhardsstrasse 6
4009 Basel, Switzerland
